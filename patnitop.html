Patnitop


Surrounded by the majestic, snow-clad mountains
of the Pir Panjal range, lush greenery and
colourful fields, Patnitop, in Jammu and
Kashmir, makes for a lovely getaway. It sits
atop a plateau in the Shivalik belt and remains
shrouded by a thick canopy of green pines and
dense woods. Cut by the pristine Chenab river,
Patnitop has mesmerising natural charms that
leave you awed. It has a number of verdant
valleys and vast, empty fields that are now being
dedicated to camps, making camping one of the most
coveted activities here.
