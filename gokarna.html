Gokarna


With stunning beaches, legendary temples
and offering several adventure sports,
Gokarna, in Karnataka, is a town with many
identities. Since antiquity, the main
attraction of the place has been its temples,
attracting devotees in thousands. It has been
known as a 'Brahmin town' and the gods have
marked not just the hills and ravines in their
names but also the beautiful beaches.
