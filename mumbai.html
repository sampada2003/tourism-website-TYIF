Mumbai


Mumbai, the capital of Maharashtra, attracts dreamers with
sky-high ambitions from every corner of the country.
It is a unique place where the past is mixed with the future
and history meets modernity, a city that lies at the cross
section of business and entertainment and beats to a
pulsating rhythm of its own. Located by the Arabian Sea,
Mumbai is an amalgamation of heritage and culture and glitz
and glamour. So from historic art deco buildings, which are
recognised by the UNESCO, to plush new-age homes of the
super rich, Mumbai has it all!
